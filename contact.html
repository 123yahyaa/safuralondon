Frequently Asked

Everything you
need to know

What is Safura London and what makes it different? +

Safura London is a British luxury modest fashion brand creating handcrafted abayas. The name Safura means beautiful in Arabic. What makes us different is simple: we make abayas the way luxury fashion houses make their finest garments — from exceptional fabrics, cut by skilled hands, with complete attention to the interior as much as the exterior. Most modest fashion is mass-produced; ours is not.

What sizes are available? +

All 12 collections are available in XS (UK 6–8 / EU 34–36), S (UK 10 / EU 38), M (UK 12–14 / EU 40–42), and L (UK 16 / EU 44). All styles are floor-length and generously cut for comfortable modest coverage. Bespoke sizing is available for the Safura Signature collection — please contact us for details.

Where are the abayas made? +

Every Safura London abaya is cut and handcrafted in our London atelier by skilled artisans. Our fabrics are sourced from certified Italian and British mills. We do not outsource manufacturing and we produce in small, controlled batches to maintain our quality standards.

What are the delivery options and costs? +

UK standard delivery is free on all orders. Express UK delivery (next business day) is £8.50. International shipping to the Gulf states (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar) is £18 and takes 3–5 working days. Worldwide shipping is available — contact us for a quote for your country. The Safura Signature collection requires 4–6 weeks as each piece is individually handcrafted.

What is your returns policy? +

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery, provided items are unworn, with all tags attached and in original packaging. Due to the handcrafted nature of our Signature collection, those pieces are non-returnable. We cover return postage costs for UK customers. For international returns, please contact us first to arrange the most efficient method.

How should I care for my Safura London abaya? +

Care varies by collection: most require dry cleaning only (we recommend this for all premium pieces to preserve fabric integrity). The Nadia collection in crepe can be gently machine washed at 30°C. Full care instructions are printed on a silk label inside each garment. When in doubt, dry clean — it is always the safer choice for luxury fabrics.

Do you offer bespoke or custom orders? +

Yes — bespoke sizing is available for the Safura Signature collection and for wholesale clients. We can also discuss custom colourways for select collections for bridal parties or large orders. Please contact us at hello@safuralondon.com with your requirements. Bespoke orders require a minimum lead time of 6–8 weeks.

Do you ship to the Gulf states, Europe, and internationally? +

Yes, we ship worldwide. Our website automatically detects your country and shows prices in your local currency (GBP, USD, EUR, AED, SAR, KWD, QAR, and more). We have many customers across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Germany, France, the Netherlands, Malaysia, Canada, and Australia. International orders are sent via tracked courier with full insurance.
